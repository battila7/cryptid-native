Complex

since:v1.0.0stability:stable
typedef struct {
  mpz_t real;
  mpz_t imaginary;
} Complex

Arbitrary precision complex number (a + bi) with integer-only real and imaginary parts.

Members

real

mpz_t

The real part a of the complex number a + bi.

imaginary

mpz_t

The imaginary part b of the complex number a + bi.

Examples

Create and Destroy Complex

New Complex instances can be created and destroyed as follows. Note, that Complex instances must be destroyed to avoid leakage of mpz_t instances.

#include "complex/Complex.h"
#include "gmp.h"

int main(int argc, char **argv) {
  Complex complex;
  
  complex_initLong(&complex, 10, 20);

  gmp_printf("Real: %Zd\nImaginary: $Zd\n", complex.real, complex.imaginary)

  complex_destroy(&complex)

  return 0;   
}

Possible Output

Real: 10
Imaginary: 20
